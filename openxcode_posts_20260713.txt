4. BUILD TIP
==================================================
When planning a new web or mobile application, a common pitfall is over-scoping the Minimum Viable Product (MVP). We often advise clients to focus on the absolute core functionality that solves a primary problem for their users.

For example, a client initially wanted to include complex data visualizations and multiple user roles in their MVP for a retail analytics platform. We helped them refine this to a simple dashboard with key performance indicators and a single administrator role. This allowed them to launch faster, gather crucial user feedback, and iterate based on real-world usage, rather than spending months building features that might not have been essential.

Rapid iteration saves resources and helps ensure you are building the right product for your audience. Our 7+ years of experience has shown that starting small and scaling smart delivers better outcomes.
